The Best Movies According To Seyi

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

The Matrix

Reality mind bender that makes you question everything with cool special effects that thrill and entertain.

Old Boy

South Korean thriller drama. Wonderfull storyline about a man who was mysteriously kidnapped and imprisoned for a decade and only realsed in order to solve the riddle of his imprisonment in the first place.

Raider of The Lost Ark

Childhood favourite, blew my mind the first time I saw it. High adventure globtotting tomb raider - infact the orginal tomb raider.
